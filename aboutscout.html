Usean Redic

Usean Redic the Creator

His Life

Usean is the founder and creator of the Super-Scouter website. He is a senior at Pinole Valley High and a member of the HGP. He lives in Richmond,CA and has lived there all his life. He desires to go to UC Berkeley or Carnegie Mellon University to study software architecture.

Why?

Usean has many sport-athlete friends, and he is one himself; being the captain of the varsity tennis team. He noticed that many of his friends wanted to play sports in college, but did not know how to get there and had no idea what they would study when they got there. Usean hopes that student-athletes across the nation can get noticed and hopefully have a higher chance of going to college and be prepared to enter(setting up a schedule, classes, and teachers).

Who?

The main audience that Usean intends to help is high school student-athletes. He also intends scouts to use the site to communicate with schools and coaches so that they may get a better understanding of the student.
